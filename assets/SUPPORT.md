This public repository does not provide:

- Service-level agreements.
- Production support guarantees.
- Access to private repositories or backend source logic.
- Customer-specific deployment guidance.
- Private telemetry, prompt, payment, token, or analytics materials.
- Automatic rights to enterprise transfer materials.

Any such work must be scoped separately.
